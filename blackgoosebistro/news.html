Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016 [cite: 19]

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. [cite: 19] In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. [cite: 20] In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching. [cite: 21]

Next month, we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at the chef table, contact us before November 30. [cite: 21, 22]
