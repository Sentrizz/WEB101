About Pawsitive Rescue.

Pawsitive Rescue was founded in 2016 by a small group of animal lovers who wanted to make a difference in the lives of abandoned and neglected pets. What began as a foster-based effort has grown into a thriving nonprofit that has helped over 1,000 animals find loving homes. Every adoption supports our work to provide medical care, training, and a second chance for animals who have nowhere else to turn.

Our Team

Emma Rodriguez – Volunteer Coordinator

Emma oversees daily volunteer operations and community events. She joined Pawsitive Rescue in 2018 and has a background in nonprofit management and animal behavior. Her favorite part of the job is seeing the joy on adopters’ faces when pets find their forever homes.

Marcus Lee – Animal Care Specialist

Marcus provides hands-on care for rescue animals and trains new volunteers on proper handling and feeding techniques. He’s been part of the rescue since its early days and is passionate about helping shy or fearful animals gain confidence.

Sofia Nguyen – Events & Outreach Lead

Sofia organizes adoption fairs and fundraising events throughout the year. With a background in marketing, she helps spread awareness about Pawsitive Rescue’s mission and connects with local partners to build community support.
